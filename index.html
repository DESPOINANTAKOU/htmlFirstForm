Section 3: Recruitment and Management of Vessel Personnel Form: O022

Seafarer Appraisal Report (Confidential)

  1. This report should be completed in full by the Master (for the deck and galley dpt.), the Chief Engineer (for the Engine dpt. ) or by the Head Office (for Master and C/E).

  2. The crewmember should be rated for all the below mentioned sections (A & B).

  3. Entries which do not relate to a section should be indicated as not applicable.

  4. The report shall be submitted to the crew department as soon as possible after the crewmember has signed-off.

  5. The Chief Engineer and the Master initiate and finalize the form.

  6. The Crew Manager and the Crew Assistant view the completed form.
